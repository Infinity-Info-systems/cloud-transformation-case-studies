# FinOps Cost Optimization Case Study

## Context

Cloud spend was growing without enough allocation visibility or optimization discipline.

## Challenge

- unclear ownership for spend
- limited tagging discipline
- underused optimization opportunities

## Intervention

- improved tagging and allocation
- rightsizing and commitment review
- regular cost governance cadence

## Outcome

- better spend visibility
- savings opportunities identified
- stronger forecast discipline

## Lessons Learned

- cost control depends on ownership
- optimization needs measurement after change
- finance and engineering must review together
