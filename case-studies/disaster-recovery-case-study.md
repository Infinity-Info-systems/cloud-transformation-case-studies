# Disaster Recovery Case Study

## Context

A critical service portfolio needed better recovery confidence and clearer evidence of readiness.

## Challenge

- incomplete recovery testing
- stale assumptions
- unclear dependency coverage

## Intervention

- documented recovery scenarios
- tested failover and restore steps
- tracked gaps to closure

## Outcome

- improved recovery confidence
- clearer ownership of recovery tasks
- better evidence for leadership review

## Lessons Learned

- recovery is only credible when it is tested
- dependency mapping matters
- evidence should be retained with the plan
