# Case Study Template

## Title

`[Transformation theme and outcome]`

## Context

- organization type
- transformation scope
- baseline state
- why the change was needed

## Challenge

- key problem
- business risk
- stakeholder impact

## Intervention

- what changed
- who was involved
- what framework or model guided the change

## Outcome

- measurable result
- business value
- operational impact

## Lessons Learned

- what worked
- what should be repeated
- what should change next time

## Evidence

- metric references
- source artifacts
- validation notes
