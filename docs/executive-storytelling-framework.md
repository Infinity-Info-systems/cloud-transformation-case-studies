# Executive Storytelling Framework

## Purpose

This framework helps turn technical transformation work into an executive-ready narrative.

## Story Structure

1. what was happening
2. why it mattered
3. what changed
4. what improved
5. what was learned

## Story Principles

- lead with business impact
- keep the language simple and direct
- avoid unnecessary implementation detail
- connect the story to leadership priorities

## Useful Story Elements

- context
- challenge
- intervention
- evidence
- result
- lesson

## Executive Questions To Answer

- What was the issue?
- Why should leaders care?
- What changed?
- What improved?
- What should happen next?
